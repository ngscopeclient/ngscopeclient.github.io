# Version 0.1.1 released

The ngscopeclient team is excited to announce the release of version 0.1.1.

This is primarily a bug-fix release, although it also adds two new instrument drivers (Batronix Magnova and SiniLink XYS3580)

This release includes contributions from 84 people across the globe and supports instruments from 25 vendors, running on all major operating systems.

[Download binaries on GitHub](https://github.com/ngscopeclient/scopehal-apps/releases/tag/v0.1.1)
